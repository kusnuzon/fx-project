📜 BBMA Oma Ally Complete Guide

🔧 INDICATORS

  • LWMA 5 & 10 High: Area Reentry Sell (Apply to High)
  • LWMA 5 & 10 Low: Area Reentry Buy (Apply to Low)
  • EMA 50: Filter Tren & Rejection Zone
  • Bollinger Bands (20): Batas Volatilitas

📊 SIGNAL CODES

  • RE (Reentry): Harga menyentuh area LWMA 5/10
  • RJ (Reject EMA 50): Harga memantul di EMA 50
  • RJB (Reject EMA+BB): Harga memantul di EMA 50 + BB (Powerful!)
  • MHV (Market Hilang Volume): Ekor sentuh BB tapi body tetap di dalam
  • EXT (Extreme): WMA 5/10 keluar dari BB

⚙️ SETUP MATCHING RULES

🟢 SWING SETUP (D1-H4-H1):

D1 RE + H4 RJ/RJB + H1 RE/EXT/MHV

🔵 INTRADAY SETUP (H4-H1-M15):

H4 RE + H1 RJ/RJB + M15 RE/EXT/MHV

🟣 SCALPING SETUP (H1-M15-M5):

H1 RE + M15 RJ/RJB + M5 RE/EXT/MHV

🔄 BBMA CYCLE

  1. EXT → WMA keluar dari BB (Harga ekstrem)
  2. TPW → Pantul ke WMA sebaliknya (Wajib TP)
  3. MHV → Gagal breakout lagi (Bensin habis)
  4. CSA → Tembus Mid BB (Sinyal awal)
  5. CSAK → Tembus Mid BB + EMA 50 (Konfirmasi)
  6. Momentum → Candle close di luar BB (Tren kuat!)
  7. Re-Entry → Balik ke WMA 5/10 (Entry point)

🎯 TARGET & STOP LOSS

  • Take Profit: High/ Low BB di timeframe signal RE
  • Stop Loss BUY: Di bawah WMA 10 Low + buffer
  • Stop Loss SELL: Di atas WMA 10 High + buffer

⚠️ GOLDEN RULES

  • ❌ No Momentum = No Re-Entry
  • ✅ Di atas EMA 50 = Fokus Buy saja
  • ✅ Di bawah EMA 50 = Fokus Sell saja
  • ⏳ Tunggu EXT → TPW → MHV selesai di TF konfirmasi
